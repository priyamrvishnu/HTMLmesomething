What is an autoimmune disease?

The mechanism of protecting against a disease within the organism is called the immune system of the body. The immune system should identify the foreign objects such as pathogens, viruses and worms and differentiate between its own healthy tissues.
The autoimmune diseases occur when the system is not able to differentiate the foreign pathogens and its own healthy tissues. As a result the immune system mistakenly attacks its own body leading to autoimmune diseases.
